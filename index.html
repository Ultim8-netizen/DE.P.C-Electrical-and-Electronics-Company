About DE.P.C Electrical and Electronics Company

Leveraging electrical and electronics expertise to create exceptional sound systems and host impactful events that inspire spiritual growth, foster community, and serve others.

Our Mission

To leverage electrical and electronics expertise to create exceptional sound systems and host impactful events that inspire spiritual growth, foster community, and serve others. With 9 years of experience, we've built our reputation on faith-driven excellence and innovative solutions.

From humble beginnings, we've grown into a nationwide recognized company specializing in audiovisual technology, event solutions, integrated media services, and multimedia production. Our commitment to excellence and service has made us a trusted partner for churches, organizations, and communities across Nigeria.

Our Core Values

  • ✓ Faith-driven excellence: We strive for technical excellence while staying true to our Christian values
  • ✓ Service-oriented: We prioritize serving others, whether through our events or sound systems
  • ✓ Community-focused: We aim to build connections and foster a sense of belonging through our work
  • ✓ Innovative solutions: We stay up-to-date with the latest technologies to deliver cutting-edge solutions

Our Journey

Key milestones and achievements that have shaped our company

2015

Humble Beginnings

Emmanuel Okereke Okwoh started as a simple electrician working under a staircase, driven by determination and a vision for excellence in electrical services.

2017-2020

Expansion into Audiovisual Services

Leveraging expertise in electrical work, the company expanded into sound system design and installation, beginning to serve churches and organizations across Nigeria.

2021-2023

Event Solutions Pioneer

Diversified services to include comprehensive event hosting, production, and multimedia solutions, establishing DE.P.C as a leader in integrated media services.

2024

Leadership Recognition

Emmanuel Okwoh appointed as director of Millennium Habitat Investment Limited, marking recognition of his business excellence and nationwide company growth.
